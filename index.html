Welcome to 5 Minute Meditation! I decided to create this site after I began to get panic attacks my junior year of college. I found that deep breathing exercises were effective in helping myself calm down during them, and wanted to create a simple and quick guided breathing exercise for myself and others to use. Thankfully, I rarely have attacks anymore, but still use this breathing exercise when feeling stressed. Hopefully you find some use in this as well, whether it be for calming down or just taking a few minutes to unwind.

-Sam
